What is Makhana?

India ka Superfood — Fox Nuts

Makhana, also called Fox Nuts or Lotus Seeds, has been part of Indian culture for thousands of years — from religious offerings to modern healthy snacking.

🌿 Harvested from Bihar's Ponds

Ancient Superfood, Modern Snacking

Makhana (Euryale ferox) grows in the slow-moving ponds and wetlands of Bihar, Assam, and Manipur. India produces over 90% of the world's makhana, making it a truly desi superfood with global recognition.

At ShildipFoods, we source our makhane directly from trusted farmers in Darbhanga, Bihar — the makhana capital of the world — ensuring freshness, quality, and fair trade with every pack.
