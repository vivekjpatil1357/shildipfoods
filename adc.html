The Mighty Makhana

Nature's crunchy gift. Ancient, roasted, and packed with plant-based protein.

Why Makhana?

Makhana, also known as Fox Nuts or Lotus Seeds, are harvested from the Euryale ferox plant. They have been used for centuries in traditional medicine and are now a global favorite for healthy snacking.
